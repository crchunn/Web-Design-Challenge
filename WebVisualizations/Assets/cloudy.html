Plot of Latitude vs. Cloudiness

If you look at this plot, you will see there isn't much difference between the North and South Hemispheres when it comes to cloudiness. There is a slight increase at 20 degrees north, but it is minimal at best. According to Wikipedia, there tends to be more cloud cover over North America, Europe and Asia, while the Middle East, Africa and Australia tend to have clearer skies.
