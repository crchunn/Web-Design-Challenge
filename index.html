Summary: Latitude with Attitudes


We want to take a few of your moments to show you visualizations of differences in weather all over the world. Primarily the differences in weather as it gets closer to the equator. The information has been pulled using APIs from OpenWeatherMap to bring together the dataset of over 500 cities randomly generated.

After we gathered the information from the different cities, we used MatPlotLib to generate scatter plots of Latitude against Temperature, Wind Speed, Humidity, and Cloudiness. With these plots, you can see how weather changes all over the world. We will provide explanations of the data correlations, as well as the source data and images.
